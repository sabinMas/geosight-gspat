# Recorded Demo Guide

Use this only as a fallback if the live demo misbehaves.

## Recording Plan

- Record the Columbia River infrastructure story first
- Record the Tokyo commercial story second
- Record the Washington residential story third

## Recording Rules

- Keep each clip short and self-contained
- Do not narrate every click; narrate the decision point
- Show the recommendation, the supporting evidence, and the source summary in every clip
- If a provider fails during recording, let the fallback path show rather than cutting around it

## Export Targets

- `geo-sight-primary-demo.mp4`
- `geo-sight-competition-backup.mp4`
- `geo-sight-competition-highlights.gif`

## What to Capture

- The Columbia River mission objective and final recommendation
- One evidence mix view showing direct, derived, and proxy labels
- One source-awareness or provenance view
- One Tokyo globality moment
- One Washington school-context moment
